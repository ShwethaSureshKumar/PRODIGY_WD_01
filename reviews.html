Review for Throne of Glass

Genre: Young Adult, Fantasy

Sarah J. Maas's "Throne of Glass" introduces readers to Celaena, a formidable assassin in a richly detailed fantasy world. The novel weaves a tale of magic, political intrigue, and self-discovery. Celaena's journey from a prison camp to a royal competition showcases Maas's skill in character development. The plot, full of mystery and magic, sets the stage for an epic series, making it a must-read for fantasy enthusiasts.
